Common Questions

How is this different from our current Agile/Scrum process?

Experiment Sprints is framework agnostic. We work within your existing ways of working, adding the specific habit of assumption-driven experimentation. Think of it as a tune-up, not a replacement.

What if we only need Sprint 0?

Perfect! Sprint 0 delivers stand-alone value. Many teams get what they need from just the initial sprint and continue independently. We're genuinely good with that.

How much time will my team need to commit?

Sprint 0 requires approximately 6 hours total (interviews + workshop). Each subsequent sprint involves up to 6 hours of facilitated work plus time for the team to run their experiments (which they determine based on their capacity).

Can this work remotely?

Absolutely. We've designed Experiment Sprints to work seamlessly in remote, hybrid, or in-person environments. All workshops use collaborative tools like Miro that your team keeps access to.

What happens if our experiment fails?

That's learning! A "failed" experiment that invalidates an assumption early saves you from investing months in the wrong direction. We help teams extract insights from every outcome and decide the best next step.

Do you work with teams outside of Europe?

Yes! We've worked with teams across multiple time zones and continents. We're flexible with scheduling to accommodate your team's location.
